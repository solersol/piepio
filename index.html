The Meme

PIEPIO is a series of memes and emotes commonly used on platforms like Discord and Twitch. They depict poorly drawn, often exaggeratedly cute versions of Pepe the Frog, a character that originated from the comic series "Boy's Club" by Matt Furie. The Peepo memes and emotes are typically used to convey various emotions and reactions in a more whimsical and less politically charged manner compared to the original Pepe the Frog memes.
